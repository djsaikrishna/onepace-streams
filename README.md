# 🏴‍☠️ Watch One Pace on Stremio (Real-Debrid & TorBox)

Experience *One Piece* the way it was meant to be watched—matching the manga, saving you hundreds of hours, and now with zero buffering.

> **🛡️ Privacy First:** This is an unofficial, community-built bridge. Your API key is **never stored or logged**. It is passed securely via your Stremio manifest URL directly to your chosen provider.

### ✨ Why Use This?
* ⚡ **Zero Buffering:** Streams route directly through your premium Debrid account.
* 🚫 **No Seeders Needed:** Instant playback, say goodbye to dead torrents.
* 🔒 **Secure Setup:** Safe, private, and seamless Stremio integration.

---

### ⚙️ Quick Install

1. Go to ➡️ [onepace-rd.vercel.app](https://onepace-rd.vercel.app/)
2. Select **Real-Debrid** or **TorBox** from the dropdown.
3. Paste your API key.
4. Click **Install Addon** to add it directly to Stremio.

---

### 🍿 How to Watch
Open Stremio, head to your **Board** (Homepage), scroll down to the **One Pace** catalog, pick an episode, and set sail! ⛵
